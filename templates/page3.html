Botball



What is Botball?

Botball is a competition that our club competes in annually, starting in February, and culminating in an April tournament.



What skills do I need to know to participate?

You do not need any skills in coding or robotics to participate! We will help you learn along the way.



What language is used for the Botball competition?

We use Robot C in the Botball competition.



What hardware is used for the Botball competition?

We use KIPR Wombats as our computers.



What does the competition entail?

In the Botball competition, we build a new competition board every year, each with different challenges for our robots to accomplish. We also work with the Wombats, servos and motors to create our robots. We compete against other teams to score the most points on our board with a pre-programmed course.
